Introduction

In many cases, getting electronics underwater may be as simple as packing a pelican case full of sensors and deploying. However, this isn't always the case, and for the few but fortunate opportunities to find yourself in such a situation, what do you do? In the past year, I began developing an electronics module that had to operate underwater in a tight form factor. As a result, I made a number of discoveries about fabricating and sealing underwater boxes.
